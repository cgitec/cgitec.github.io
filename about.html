About CGITEC

Visualizing Radiation, Enhancing Safety.

Our Mission

CGITEC (씨지텍) is a pioneer in advanced radiation detection and imaging technologies. Based in Daejeon, South Korea, we specialize in visualizing the invisible—turning complex radiation data into clear, actionable visual information.

Our comprehensive solutions include real-time gamma-ray spectrum analysis, nuclide identification, and 3D mapping.

We serve critical industries including Nuclear Power, Defense, Medical, and Environmental Safety, providing tools that ensure safety and efficiency in the most demanding environments.
